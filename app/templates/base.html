Methodology & assumptions
  • z-score: z = (rhorizon − μdaily·h) / (σdaily·√h), trailing daily μ/σ over the vol window.
  • Horizons (non-overlapping): 1-week = last 5 td; 1-month-ex-week = day −21 to −5.
  • Reversion: distance from 20d mean (P−SMA20)/SMA20; RSI(14); MACD(12,26,9).
  • Ranking: by |composite z| in both tails (we do not rank toward the median).
  • Idiosyncratic vs sector: peer-relative z = stock z − GICS sub-industry peer median z; |·| ≥ threshold ⇒ idiosyncratic.
  • Indicators computed in-app from uploaded FactSet price/volume — no external market-data calls.
Educational tool. Not investment advice.
